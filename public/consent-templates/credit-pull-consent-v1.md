# DRAFT — Credit Report Soft-Pull Authorization (v1.0)

I authorize {{bank_name}} and its service providers to obtain a copy of my
consumer credit report for the purpose of evaluating my application for
SBA financing. I understand that:

- This is a **soft inquiry**. It does not affect my credit score and is
  not visible to other lenders as a hard inquiry.
- {{bank_name}} may use the information in this report, together with
  other information I provide, to evaluate my creditworthiness for this
  application.
- If {{bank_name}} extends an offer of credit, it may separately request
  my authorization for a full ("hard") credit pull as part of its formal
  underwriting and adverse-action process. That is a distinct
  authorization from this one.

By checking the box and submitting, I am providing my written
instruction under the Fair Credit Reporting Act, 15 U.S.C. § 1681b(a)(2).
