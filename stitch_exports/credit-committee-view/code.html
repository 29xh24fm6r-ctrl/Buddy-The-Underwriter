Executive Summary

Request: Titan Equities (Sponsor) requests a $42.5MM senior bridge loan to acquire and renovate "Project Atlas," a 210-unit mixed-use asset in Austin's CBD. The sponsor is acquiring the asset at a basis of $215k/unit, approx. 20% below replacement cost.

Merits: The asset is located in a prime growth corridor with 98% market occupancy. Sponsor has a strong track record in the Austin market (4 successful exits >20% IRR). The floating rate structure allows for a flexible exit via refi or sale upon stabilization in 24-36 months.

Risks & Mitigants: Key risk is the current occupancy (72%) due to ongoing renovation turnover. Mitigated by an upfront $2.5MM interest reserve and a structured earnout of $4.0MM conditioned on achieving 1.25x DSCR for two consecutive quarters.
