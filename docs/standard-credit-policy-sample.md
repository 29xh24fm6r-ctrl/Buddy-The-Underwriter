# Standard Credit Policy (Sample)

> Use this as a test policy document for Phase 2: Policy-Aware Underwriting.
> 
> **Note:** This is not legal advice. It’s intentionally simplified and written to exercise DSCR/LTV/FICO/cash injection/loan amount rules.

## 1) Global Policy Principles
- Underwriting must be **document-driven** and **policy-grounded**.
- Exceptions require documented justification and appropriate approvals.
- Where a metric is not available, the deal should be flagged for missing evidence.

## 2) Commercial Real Estate (CRE)
### 2.1 Loan-to-Value (LTV)
- **Maximum LTV:** 80% of appraised value.
- If LTV exceeds 80%:
  - Require additional collateral or reduce loan amount.
  - Consider stronger guarantor support.

### 2.2 Debt Service Coverage Ratio (DSCR)
- **Minimum DSCR:** 1.25x for stabilized CRE.
- DSCR may be considered at **1.15x** only with compensating factors:
  - Strong guarantor liquidity
  - Additional collateral
  - Strong tenant quality / long-term leases

## 3) SBA 7(a)
### 3.1 Loan Amount
- **Maximum SBA 7(a) loan amount:** $5,000,000.

### 3.2 DSCR
- **Minimum global DSCR:** 1.15x.
- If DSCR is below 1.15x, mitigants are required and the deal may require exception approval.

### 3.3 Minimum Credit Score
- **Minimum FICO:** 680.
- If FICO is 660–679:
  - Consider additional guarantor strength or increased equity injection.

## 4) Term Loans (Non-SBA)
### 4.1 Maximum Amount
- **Maximum term loan amount without executive approval:** $5,000,000.

### 4.2 Borrower Strength
- Target minimum FICO is 660.

## 5) Owner-Occupied Real Estate
- **Minimum cash injection:** 10%.
- If cash injection is below 10%:
  - Consider seller financing, documented gift funds, or additional collateral.

## 6) Equipment Financing
- **Minimum FICO:** 660.
- **Maximum equipment LTV:** 85%.

## 7) Exception Governance
- Exceptions must include:
  - Policy rule violated
  - Measured value and policy threshold
  - Mitigants and compensating factors
  - Required approvals
